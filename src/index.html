RiffReady

As part of my final year of university, I created a web application that enables a guitarist to play along to with a song by linking Spotify and Ultimate Guitar. I used Spotify's Web Developer SDK to link to a user's account and retrieve plaback information, and then scraped Ultimate Guitar to retrieve the most appropriate tablature or chord sheet using that data.
